I am a PhD student specializing in human development at UC Irvine's School of Education. My research primarily examines how family relationships shape girls' motivational development in STEM fields. I'm particularly interested in leveraging advanced quantitative methods to capture the complexity of these processes as they unfold across adolescence and young adulthood. 

You can find more about my research on my [Publications](/publications/) and [CV](/cv/) pages.
